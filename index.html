Elegance and
endurance unite
in the Omega
Seamaster

The Omega Seamaster is a legendary timepiece that embodies precision, durability, and style. With its origins dating back to 1948, this iconic collection was designed for those with an adventurous spirit. It combines elegant design with advanced technology, making it suitable for both underwater exploration and daily wear.

MOVEMENT

CALIBRE
OMEGA 8900

Self-winding movement with Co-Axial escapement. Certified
Master Chronometer, approved by METAS, resistant to magnetic
fields reaching 15,000 gauss. Free sprung-balance with silicon balance
spring, two barrels mounted in series, automatic winding in both
directions. Time zone function. Special luxury finish with rhodium-plated
rotor and bridges with Geneva waves in arabesque.
